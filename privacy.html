Privacy Policy for YelTimer

At YelTimer, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard the information you provide when using our timer application.

Information Collection and Use

YelTimer does not collect any personal information from its users. We do not require you to provide any personal data such as your name, email address, or location to use our timer application.

Data Storage

YelTimer does not store any personal data or usage data on our servers. All timer data is stored locally on your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

Third-party Services

YelTimer does not integrate with any third-party services that may collect personal information. We do not use any analytics or tracking tools in our application.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update or change our Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes to the Privacy Policy will be reflected on this page. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any updates or changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at contact@yeltimer.com.
